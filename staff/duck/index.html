Hello all, Duck here! Thanks for using Snoonet!

I’m part of the operations team, being primarily responsible for maintaining our server infrastructure and making sure things are up-to-date, as well as assisting the staff team with rule & guideline enforcement across the network as needed. I’m also involved with the InspIRCd project, whose software powers this network.

When I’m not doing network-related things, I can usually be found chatting in a handful of the communities that Snoonet hosts. If you see me, feel free to say hi, or send me a message at any time using /msg Duck Hi!
